The Crane Wives

This group have made music that are similair to these genres: Indie Rock, Americana and Folk. The members, Emilee Petersmark(guitar, vocals), Kate Pillsbury(guitar, vocals), Dan Rickabus(drums, vocals) and Ben Zito(bass) have been working together since 2010. A former member, Tom Gunnels who played the banjo, has left the band in 2015. the Reason for this is still unknown.

To this day, the band is still actively making music. One of the more recent ones, Arcturus Beaming, only being made a year ago, but since then they havent produced any more songs. They have produced 7 Albums over the years.

I have nothing else to talk about other than my favourite songs from this band. So, my favourite songs are "Tongues and Teeth", "The moon will sing", "Curses" and "Take me to War". -Cady
